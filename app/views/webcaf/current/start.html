Submit a WebCAF self-assessment form

The WebCAF self-assessment form allows organisations to assess their critical systems as part of GovAssure, the cyber assurance process that is being used to understand the level of cyber resilience across government.

WebCAF is used at stage 3 of GovAssure and is based on the National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Assurance Framework (CAF).

As part of stage 4, self-assessment forms on WebCAF will undergo an independent assurance review or a peer review.

In stage 5, organisations will receive a report including observations, recommendations and an assessment against the target CAF profile for each system.

Before you start

You must have completed stage 1 and stage 2 of GovAssure, which includes::

  • having documented all systems that will be assessed in a scoping document
  • knowing whether each system will be assessed against the Baseline or Enhanced Government CAF profile
  • having a GovAssure coordinator lead role in place to manage completion of the self-assessment(s)
