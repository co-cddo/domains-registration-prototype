Submit a CAF self-assessment for a system

GovAssure is the cyber security assurance scheme for assessing the critical systems of government organisations.

GovAssure uses the NCSC's Cyber Assurance Framework (CAF). GovAssure is currently designed for OFFICIAL government systems, and also applies to government sector critical national infrastructure (CNI).

CAF government profiles

To enable an effective approach to dealing with the different threats there are two CAF government profiles for government. These are:

  • Baseline profile
  • Enhanced profile

{{ govukWarningText({ html: 'All government organisations must meet the Baseline profile. If your organisation wants to assign the Enhanced profile to a system, you should contact us at cybergovassure@cabinetoffice.gov.uk.', iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

How long it takes

Submitting a CAF self-assessment should take approximately 30 minutes.

The CAF self-assessment will be reviewed and verified by an independent assessor. A final report is produced, including observations, recommendations, and an assessment against the target CAF profile for each system. You can learn more about the GovAssure 5 stages.

Before you start

You must have the scoping document completed with:

  • Description of the organisation's context and mission
  • Listing the systems in-scope for CAF self-assessment with assigned Government CAF profiles for each
