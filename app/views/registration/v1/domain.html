What .gov.uk domain name do you want?

A .gov.uk domain name must clearly describe your registrant’s organisation or government initiative. For example a:

  • central department can use hmrc.gov.uk
  • county council can use northamptonshire.gov.uk
  • town council can use biggleswadetowncouncil.gov.ukor biggleswadetown.gov.uk
  • parish councils can use bonbyparishcouncil.gov.uk, bonbyparish.gov.uk or bonby-pc.gov.uk
