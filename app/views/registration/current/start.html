Use this service to:

  • check if you are eligible to register a .gov.uk domain name
  • submit a registration request for a .gov.uk domain
  • get a filtered list of approved registrars who work with parish councils

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Who can apply

You can register a .gov.uk domain if you are:

  • central government department or agency - unless you qualify for an exemption
  • non-departmental body - also known as an arm’s length body
  • fire service
  • county, borough, metropolitan or district council
  • parish, town or community council
  • neighbourhood or village council
  • combined or unitary authority
  • police and crime commissioner
  • joint authority
  • joint committee
  • partnership body

What you can register for

A top level domain name (TLD), specifically a .gov.uk domain name, that is managed through a dedicated UK registry operator.
