What is the requested .gov.uk domain name?

A .gov.uk domain name must clearly describe the Registrant organisation or government initiative. For example:

  • a central department can use hmrc.gov.uk
  • a county council can use northamptonshire.gov.uk
  • a town council can use biggleswadetowncouncil.gov.uk
  • a parish council can use bonbyparishcouncil.gov.uk.
