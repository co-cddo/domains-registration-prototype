What happens next

  1. We will contact you either to confirm approval or to ask for more information within five working days.
  2. To confirm approval, we will send you a unique code, which you will need to use to complete the registration.
  3. A .gov.uk domain must be registered with the registry operator (Nominet), after it has received approval from the Domains Team. The .gov.uk domain is live on the internet once you have registered.

How appeals work

If the Domains Team rejects an application you can appeal on behalf of your registrant by emailing appeals@domains.gov.uk.

We will forward this appeal with any additional information we request or your registrant wants to provide to the Naming and Approvals Committee (NAC) and keep you up-to-date with progress.

The NAC will make a final decision and will give its reasoning.
