What happens next

We've sent your application to the Domains Management team.

They will contact you either to confirm approval of the chosen domain name registration or to ask for more information within five working days.

If your application is rejected, you can choose to appeal to the decision.

Contact your registrar

We have selected a list of suitable suppliers who can help you with your domain registration.

Registrars are suppliers who deal with the domain name setup on DNS servers. After you have download a list of approved registrars, you need to choose a preferred supplier who will assist you, based on your needs.

Share your domain details with your registrar

  1. Give the share code and your registered office address postcode to the supplier who you have appointed as your registrar.
  2. To see that your .gov.uk domain name has been approved, they must enter the share code and your registered office address postcode at www.gov.uk/check-gov-uk-domain-details
  3. Contact your registrar to make sure they have all the information they need.
