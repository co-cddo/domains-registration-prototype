Get approval to use a .gov.uk domain name

Use this service to:

  • do something
  • do something
  • do something else

Applying for approval takes around 5 minutes.

Who can apply for approval

You can apply for approval if you are:

  • a .gov.uk Approved Registrar
  • applying for a .gov.uk domain name on behalf of your Registrant

How long it takes to get approval

The application process should take approximately 5 working days. We may ask for more information during this time and this may delay the process. Our processing times will also depend on the volume and complexity of applications currently in progress.

Before you start

You will need:

  • to check your Registrant’s organisation can get a .gov.uk domain name
  • to check the proposed domain meets the choose your .gov.uk domain name criteria
  • to upload signed permission from the person who will be accountable for the .gov.uk domain name
  • an exemption from using the GOV.UK website if your Registrant is from a central government organisation or ALB
  • the name, role, organisation, and contact details of your Registrant
  • the name, role, organisation, and contact details of your Applicant, if they are different from the Registrant
