Get approval to use a .gov.uk domain name

The Domains Team must approve all .gov.uk domain names before they can be registered.

Who can apply for approval

Use this service if you're a .gov.uk Approved Registrar and you want to apply for a .gov.uk domain on behalf of your registrant.

{{ govukInsetText({ text: "If you are a registrant you must choose a .gov.uk Approved Registrar and pay them to make this application on your behalf." }) }}

How long it takes to get approval

The approval process should take approximately 5 working days. We may ask for more information during this time and this may delay the process. Our processing times will also depend on the volume and complexity of applications currently in progress.

Before you start

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "You must be a .gov.uk Approved Registrar to use this service.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

You will need to:

  • check your registrant's organisation can get a .gov.uk domain name
  • check the proposed domain name meets the choose your .gov.uk domain name rules
  • have signed permission ready to upload from the person who will be accountable for the domain name
  • get an exemption from using the GOV.UK website if your registrant is from a central government organisation or arm's-length bodies (ALBs)
  • have the name, role, organisation and contact details of your registrant
  • have the name, role, organisation and contact details of your applicant, if they are different from the registrant
